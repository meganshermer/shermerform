My name is Megan Shermer and I’m a graphic designer. I’ve realized a lot of designers have a self-proclaimed art style and although I tend to lean more toward specific mediums and art direction I’ve come to the realization that sticking to one medium, style, or direction is simply boring. You will see that a lot of my work differs in similarities or will look the same and I hope it entertains you as much as it does for me.
